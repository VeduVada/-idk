CELESTIAL ARTISTRY

Art event

Unleash your creativity and join us for an exhilarating art competition like no other!
Whether you're a seasoned artist or just love to doodle, this event is all about celebrating your unique style and letting your imagination run wild.

Picture a day where your imagination takes center stage, with every corner buzzing with excitement and artistic flair. With fun challenges, exciting prizes, and a lively atmosphere,
this competition is not just about winning—it's about enjoying the process and connecting with fellow art enthusiasts. With exciting prizes and a community that celebrates every stroke and splash,
this is more than a competition; it's an unforgettable experience filled with inspiration, joy, and boundless fun!

Grab your brushes, pencils, and passion, put on your creative hat, and let's create something amazing together!


Come explore, engage, and experience the beauty of art at Celestial Artistry.

Rules

  • Work should be completed within the allotted time.
  • Participants are expected to share the work equally among team members.
  • If the artwork is not related to the topic, the contestant will be eliminated.
  • No copying, referencing, or inspiration from others will be allowed.
  • Participants must bring their own art supplies and materials as needed after viewing the themes.
  • Only paper will be provided by the organizers.

EventOutline

  • Two rounds will be held for Category B. Round 1 will be held on the first day and Round 2 will be held on the second day.
  • Finalists of Round 1 will be announced on Google Classrooms, which will be created soon after registrations.
  • Round 1
    • Students can only participate individually.
    • Topic – Life on Mars
    • Time – 80 mins
    • A description/paragraph on the topic ‘Life on Mars’ will be given to the contestants. It is up to the participants to include the given description in their artworks using their creativity.
    • Participants will be judged based on:
      • Creativity
      • Color utilization
      • Overall presentation
      • Artistic skills
      • Inclusivity of given description
    • Participants must bring their own art materials. Only paper will be provided by the organizers.
    • The 80-minute time limit must be strictly followed.
    • The finalists of Round 1 will be announced on Google Classrooms, which will be created shortly after registrations close.
  • Round 2
    • Round 2 is a team event (2 members). The teams will be decided by the judges and will be announced in the Google Classroom shortly after Round 1.
    • Topic will be announced on the day of the event.
    • Time – 80 minutes
    • Each team consists of two members.
    • Each team will have to choose a chit, which will contain the name of a random object that they will have to incorporate in their artworks, using their creativity, while making sure to follow the given topic.
    • Participants will be judged based on:
      • Creativity
      • Color utilization
      • Overall presentation
      • Artistic skills
      • Inclusivity of given object
      • Teamwork
    • Participants must bring their own art materials. Only paper will be provided by the organizers.
    • The 80-minute time limit must be strictly followed.
