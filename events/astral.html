ASTRAL FOCUS

Photography event

Discover the Stars: Welcome to Astral Focus!

This is your chance to shine and be recognized for your photographic artistry!

Unleash your imagination and express your unique perspective through the lens.

Students are welcome to participate, from middle school enthusiasts to high school shutterbugs. Whether you're a budding photographer or a seasoned pro, we want to see your vision of the universe!

Be part of a vibrant community of young photographers who share your passion for capturing the beauty of nature. Connect with fellow participants, exchange ideas, and be inspired by the wonders of the universe.

Get Started Today!

Rules

  • Participants can bring cameras or phones to take photos (with a note from parents).
  • The judges/organizers/school are not responsible for any damage to equipment; it is the sole responsibility of participants.
  • No inappropriate photos/poses will be allowed on campus. If found with such content, participants will be immediately disqualified.
  • Participants must bring their own SD cards and/or card readers.
  • After every round, participants will have to upload the photos taken onto the computers provided by the school. All photos must be wiped from the cameras/phones.
  • Any attempt to bring footage from home will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Participants are not allowed to copy/take inspiration from any source. Internet access will be cut off for the entire duration of the event.
  • Participants are advised not to bring lights/diffusers or any such equipment. The organizers will provide these upon request.
  • Students can carry props related to their theme ( only for the second round ).

Event outline

  • Students can participate individually .
  • There will be three rounds, each with topics given on the spot.
  • Participants can be grouped into teams at the judges' discretion.
  • Students will be judged based on creativity, relevance to the theme, quality, and composition.
  • Students can carry props related to their theme ( only for the second round for category c).
  • The first round will be an individual photography round.
  • The second round will be a marketing round.
  • Students can carry props related to their theme ( only for the second round ).
  • The third round will have a dynamic topic that can change based on the previous two rounds.

Camera requirement


DSLR camera ( mirror or mirrorless )
2. should be a basic lens ( 18 - 55mm , 16 - 300mm , 35mm - 85mm , 85mm - 135mm , 35mm - 200mm , should within the range of 14 - 300 m )
