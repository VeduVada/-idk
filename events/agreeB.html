AGREE TO DISAGREE

Debate event

Hey Naruto fans, Shahrukh Khan lovers, and Hollywood buffs! Welcome to "Agree to Disagree," the ultimate rapid-fire game where your favorite anime and movies collide!

Picture this: you're trying to describe Naruto's epic battle scenes or a classic Shahrukh Khan moment—without using any names. Can you get your friends to guess Goku’s Kamehameha or Leonardo DiCaprio’s most iconic lines with just a few clues and expressions?

Whether you’re a fan of Attack on Titan, a Marvel maniac, or you love Bollywood’s biggest hits, this is your chance to show off your quick thinking and creativity. Get ready for non-stop laughs, fast-paced fun, and some seriously epic moments.

Rules

  • Each participant can get another partner belonging to the same house colour.
  • Time: 3 hrs
  • Team Event: 2 in a team
  • The participants must be courteous to other participants, the judges, and teachers.
  • The topics will be intimated any time before the event.
  • The participants must speak in turn. Any use of foul/derogatory language will result in immediate disqualification.
  • The judges have the right to silence any participant to allow for fair debate.
  • Participants can be asked to flip sides in the debate if required.

Event outline

  • Event Structure:
    • ROUND 1: Rapid-Fire Balloon Burst
      • Objective: Teams will guess movies, anime, or songs played by the judges.
      • Rules:
        • Guessing Game: Judges will play clips from popular movies, anime like Naruto or Attack on Titan, songs from Bollywood hits like those of Shahrukh Khan, or Hollywood classics.
        • Balloon Burst: Each team will be given a balloon. When they know the answer, they must burst their balloon to answer the question.
        • Points System: Points are awarded for correct answers and how confidently the team answers.
        • Silence Rule: Teams must maintain silence unless they are bursting a balloon to answer. Unnecessary balloon bursts or noise will result in a point deduction and possible disqualification.
    • ROUND 2: Speak Up!
      • Objective: Teams will speak on a topic related to movies, anime, or famous actors.
      • Rules:
        • Topic Selection: Teams will choose a topic through a game played on the same evening of ROUND 1.
        • Team Presentation: Each team will have 1 minute to speak on their chosen topic, dividing the time equally between both members.
        • Scoring: Teams will be judged based on their content, expression, and how well they manage their time.
    • Judging Criteria:
      • Accuracy: Correctness of the answers in Round 1.
      • Confidence: How confidently teams answer in Round 1 and present in Round 2.
      • Expression: Creativity and expression in describing or acting out scenes.
      • Teamwork: Collaboration between team members, especially in Round 2.
    • Judging:
      • Best Debater
      • Honourable Mention
      • Special Mention
