SUPERNOVA

Fashion show

Get ready to strut your stuff on the catwalk!
NHVPS is thrilled to announce its upcoming fashion show SUPERNOVA, where you will have the chance to unleash your creativity and style.

Choose from a range of captivating themes, allowing you to express your unique fashion sensibilities. Whether you're a trendsetter, a visionary designer, or a budding model, there's a place for you on our runway.

So, dust off those sketchbooks, raid your closets for inspiration, and get ready to make a statement.
NHVPS’ Fashion Show, SUPERNOVA awaits, where creativity knows no bounds!

Rules

  • NO INAPPROPRIATE CLOTHES:
    • No sleeveless
    • No shorts
    • No skirts above knee level
    • No see-through clothes
    • No deep neckline clothes
    • No backless
    • No shirtless
    • No crop-tops
  • Participants are expected to be dressed from their house
  • Participants are required to change into their school uniform right after the event.

Event outline

  • PARTICIPATION
    • CATERGORY A: only solo participation
    • CATERGORY B: both solo and duo participation permissible
    • CATERGORY C: both solo and duo participation permissible
  • Participants MUST be ready with outfits for both rounds
  • There will be two rounds: Round 1 on the first day and Round 2 (FINALS) on the second day.
  • Participants will be given different themes to choose from. One team can only choose one theme per round. THE THEME CANNOT BE REPEATED FOR ROUND 2. Participants MUST BE READY WITH OUTFITS FOR BOTH DAYS.
  • Students will be judged based on creativity, relevance to the theme, the walk, and confidence.
  • Category A and Category B will be expected to dress up from their house.
  • Category C has to bring their outfits along with accessories to the school. They will be given an hour to dress up at school. They must send their audios 3 days before the event and carry the same on a pen drive on the day of the event.
  • Students can carry props related to their theme.
  • There will be a GOLDEN BUZZER awarded to two teams or participants in each category who have the best outfit, walk, and presentation.
  • Participants are required to carry their school uniform with them and will have to change back into uniform after the event.
