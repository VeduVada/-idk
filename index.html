General rules

  • No Use of AI Tools:

    Participants are not allowed to utilize any artificial intelligence tools or platforms during the preparation or presentation of their pitches.

  • Internet Usage Restrictions:

    Participants may only use the internet during the event when explicitly permitted by the event organizers. Unauthorized internet usage, including accessing external resources or communication platforms without permission, is prohibited.

  • Adherence to Event Schedule:

    Participants must adhere to the schedule provided by the event organizers, including presentation times, breaks, and deadlines for submission of materials. Failure to comply with the schedule may result in penalties or disqualification.

  • Compliance with Rules and Regulations:

    Participants are required to comply with all rules and regulations set forth by the event organizers ,failure to comply may result in penalties, disqualification, or other disciplinary actions as deemed appropriate by the organizers.

  • Code of Ethics:

    Participants are expected to uphold high ethical standards throughout the event, including honesty, integrity, and fairness in all interactions and presentations.

  • Event Organizer's Authority:

    The event organizers reserve the right to interpret and enforce these rules as they see fit, and to take appropriate action in cases of rule violations or misconduct, including but not limited to warnings, penalties, disqualification, or expulsion from the event.
